03 — Advertising

Ads and consent

KittyMystery is free to play and supported by optional rewarded ads powered by Google AdMob. A rewarded ad plays between levels — you can always choose not to watch it.

When you first open the app, we will ask for your consent before showing personalised ads. If you are in the EU/EEA, this consent is required under GDPR. You can change your choice at any time in the app settings.

If you do not consent, non-personalised ads may still be shown. AdMob may collect the following for ad delivery: device identifiers, IP address (approximate location), app usage data, and ad interaction data.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

04 — Legal basis (GDPR)

Why are we allowed to process your data?

Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — for personalised advertising via AdMob. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — for crash reporting and app stability. We have a legitimate interest in keeping the app working correctly.

Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — for game progress and leaderboard scores, which are necessary to provide the game service you requested.

06 — Retention

How long do we keep your data?

Game progress is stored locally on your device and deleted when you uninstall the app. We do not retain personal data on our own servers.

Google AdMob and Apple Game Center retain data according to their own privacy policies, linked above.

08 — Children

Children's privacy

KittyMystery is rated 4+ and designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to provide the game. Parental consent is handled through Apple's Family Sharing and Screen Time controls.

Personalised advertising is disabled for users identified as children.

10 — Manage Consent

Withdraw or change your advertising consent

You gave consent for personalised advertising when you first opened KittyMystery. You can withdraw or change this consent at any time — it will not affect anything that happened before you change your mind.

1
Open KittyMystery on your iPhone or iPad.
2
Tap the ⚙️ gear icon in the top-right corner of the puzzle screen.
3
Tap "Manage Ad Preferences" — the Google consent form will open.
4
Choose "Do not consent" or adjust your preferences and confirm.
ℹ️

If you withdraw consent, you will see non-personalised ads instead. The game remains fully free to play either way. Your choice applies only to this device and this app.
